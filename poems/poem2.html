Lost in the world
Im searching for words
But my vision is blurred
These emotions are absurd

I cannoot explain
The feelings i attain
Maybe i'm insane
But i'm probably just in pain

It could be miscommunication
Or severe dehydration
But it's always an expensive citation
When im around your location

Your sweet little lies
Your devious spies
All tranquilize
My intensions to apologize

Tomorrow i'll wake
Another mistake i'll make
But believe it's not fake
When i speak of this ache

Lost in the world
I'm still searching for words
Im just a nerd
Singing my songs about a black bird
