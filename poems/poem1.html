Big blue Sky
Do you see when i cry
Do you see them lie
Everytime i try

Maybe im a bad guy
For not wanting to crucify
Those who attempt to clarify
The problems we all deny

I look around and i sigh
Because i know money can't buy
My desire to fly
Or my want to beautify

Can they not see
Your subtle plea
To not live care-free
And not eat fruit from the tree

I know love is the key
The way to be set free
The way to reassemble the debris
That we've left in the sea
