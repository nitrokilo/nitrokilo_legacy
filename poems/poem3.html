Every day I wake
Every breathe I take
A subtle felling I get
Because I am in debt

Not a financial debt
If that were the case, I would be a cadet
A debt to myself, this burden is
A painful feeling it presents, like that of a pop quiz

No one likes a pop quiz
Same way no one likes their soda with extra fizz
This matter clouds my mind
So much so I swear I’m going blind

Okay maybe I’m not going blind
I just feel hella confined
Like I ain’t a mastermind
Sometimes I feel like I gotta save humankind

Why do I have to save humankind?
Like is it so hard to show love and leave the hate behind?
Like why can’t we love one another?
Why can’t we treat everyone like they’re our brother?

Even if you don’t have a biological brother
I know you love your mother
Why do we hate?
Do we think it’s the ultimate checkmate?

I promise you it’s not a checkmate
It’s really just an excuse for you not to accommodate
I’m saying spread love and share the cake
I promise you it’s not going to hurt like a bite from a snake

Tomorrow I shall wake
Many more breaths I hope to take
A world with more love I hope we exist in
Or else all our efforts would have ended in the bin
