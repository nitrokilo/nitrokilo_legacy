Why Is Your Name?

Your name is who you are, who you were ,and who you will be. A group of letters is the only universal definition of your existence. Our physical form will fade and our spirits will move elsewhere. Therefore, our existence on this planet can and will always be defined by our name. Your name is important. Be your name, breathe your name, believe in your name.
