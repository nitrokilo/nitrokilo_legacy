Timeforce


Productivity Redefined


Who is Timeforce?


Timeforce is a minimalistic command line, multi input countdown timer with the sole purpose of increasing productivity.


Why is Timeforce?


Rotating between social media apps and work/studying is a dangerous game. A game that often leads to the all to familiar feeling of unproductivity and disappointment. Time management is hard skill to master, regardless of if you’re a junior in high-school or Chief Operations Officer of a multinational company.


I mean everyone wants to know what crypto Elon is pumping or how many likes that new Instagram post got, but if it comes at the cost of making a 30 min paper turn into a 50min paper, is it worth it?

The answer is most probably

NO!


What is Timeforce?


Timeforce is comprised of 3 sections. A hot timer, a chill timer and a rotations manager. The hot timer is the first countdown timer, it counts down the user input plays a unique sound to indicate the end of first timer then begins the second timer, counting down the other user input then plays another unique sound. The rotation manager controls how many times each timer will run.
